### Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, the game offers a vast and detailed world filled with missions, activities, vehicles, and opportunities for exploration.

Players experience the story through three unique protagonists whose lives become intertwined through a series of dangerous heists and criminal ventures.

* 🎮 First-person and third-person gameplay

## Gameplay

GTA V combines exploration, driving, shooting, and storytelling into a single immersive experience. Players can freely roam the world, complete missions, participate in races, invest in businesses, and discover countless side activities.

## Story

The game's story follows Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips as they navigate the criminal underworld while facing powerful enemies, government agencies, and personal conflicts.

## Open World

Los Santos and Blaine County offer one of the most detailed open worlds in gaming. From bustling city streets to remote deserts and mountain ranges, every area is filled with unique locations and activities.

## Grand Theft Auto Online

Grand Theft Auto Online expands the experience with multiplayer gameplay, allowing players to create characters, purchase properties, complete missions, and participate in events with friends and players worldwide.

## Why Play GTA V?

Grand Theft Auto V delivers an unmatched combination of storytelling, freedom, and open-world gameplay. Its engaging narrative, diverse activities, and continuously evolving online mode have made it one of the most popular video games ever created.
